All About ME!

Hannah Golob

My Story

  I was born in sunny southern on California December 12th, just three
  days after my parents first anniversary. I consider myself their first
  gift.

  I have one sibling. My brother is roughly 2 and a half years younger
  than me. He and I are extremely close. We love to watch movies together
  and play video games. He always beats me in Mario Kart .

Fun Facts!

  When I was four I moved to Idaho. Because I was so young I don't
  remember what California was like before I left.
  But a brief 12 years later I moved right back to the same town I was
  born in.

  My name is a palindrome. H A N N A H. It can be spelled forward
  and backward. In kindergarten I was probably the only kid who knew
  the word "palindrome" because my dad taught it to me and was adamant
  I knew what it meant.

  I absolutely love peppermint tea!
  Over winter break I found a huge stash in my house and started having a cup
  at least twice a day. Now at school, my roommate and I have a full-blown
  tea collection. I thrifted this cute green mug over break. I have at least one
  cup of peppermint tea a day in it!
